Allergen Information

Your safety is our priority. Please review our dietary guide below.

Important Safety Notice

While we take every precaution to identify ingredients, Momma's BBQ prepares all food in a common kitchen area. Cross-contamination may occur, and we cannot guarantee that any item is 100% free of allergens.

Peanut Oil

We use Peanut Oil in our fryers. This affects the following items:

  • Fried Pickles
  • Half Chicken
  • Chicken Wings
  • French Fries

Gluten Info

Most of our smoked meats are prepared without gluten. However, the following items DO contain gluten:

  • Meatloaf
  • Fried Pickles
  • Cornbread
  • Mac n Cheese
  • Cheezy Corn
  • Gravy

Dairy & Eggs

Contains Milk: Mac n Cheese, Cheezy Corn, Slaw, Potato Salad, Cornbread, Mashed Potatoes, Ice Cream, and Bread Pudding.

Contains Egg: Our signature Momma's Mustard contains egg.
